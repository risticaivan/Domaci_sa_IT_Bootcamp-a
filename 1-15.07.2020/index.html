Something about me


Volleyball

More than a third of my life I have been playing volleyball. As a child, I had fun a lot, and meet some people that are some of my close friends today. Thru all my carrier I never give up even when I had some ups and downs. I played in "OK Niš" more than 6 years and couldn't ask for a better team. Last season I played, with them, we won the first league in Serbia and got a chance to play in Superleague. Due to my financial problems I had to drop from my team and find a job. My love for sports never disappeared even when I had surgery. The most important thing I have learned from playing volleyball is that "one player doesn't make a whole team".

Hobby

I love to play video games because in some way they make me relax and clear my head. I used to play Ikariam online and I wanted to edit some page in the game. That was the first time I meet HTML. I didn't know what it was but I got good at it and liked to tamper with it. It got really interesting at the end, and that's why I wanted to learn more about it. I started to code and really enjoyed it. Even if it gives me a headache I love to see the finished product. And that's what motivates me to code and learn more. The second hobby I have is to find more about electronics. I love IT and its future. When I check and an item I love to see the specs and go digging deep to find the last bit of truth about it. I'm sure that in the future I will have more hobbies but for now, I can say that these two are the only ones I have.

Interests

I hope that in the future I will have more opportunities to pursue my interest. I like motorcycles, sports, new hardware, programs, video games and more.
